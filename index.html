Smart City for the Elderly

Topic 1



Proposed service that allows the user to listen to music on their phone and control it by thought, all that’s needed are the silicone ear piece or the headphones. Electrodes in the devices record the user’s brain activity and send it to their phone via Bluetooth, which translates it into a device command. Playing songs, creating playlists and searching for new music has never been this easy!
